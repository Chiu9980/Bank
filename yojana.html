🤝 SCSS Bank – Government Yojanas & Schemes

🌾 PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Direct income support of ₹6,000 annually to small and marginal farmers through bank transfer.

  • Eligibility: Land-owning farmers
  • Documents: Aadhaar, land records, bank account

👧 Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

Saving scheme for girl child offering high interest and tax benefits for future education and marriage.

  • Higher interest than regular savings
  • Withdrawal allowed after 18 years
  • Eligibility: Girl child below 10 years with guardian
  • Documents: Birth certificate, Aadhaar of guardian, initial deposit

🏥 Ayushman Bharat Yojana

Health protection scheme offering ₹5 lakh annual coverage per family for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

  • Eligibility based on SECC data
  • SCSS Bank helps with scheme registration and linking accounts
  • Documents: Aadhaar, Ration Card, Income Certificate

💼 Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

Loan scheme to promote small business enterprises under 3 categories:

  • Shishu: Loans up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: Loans ₹50,001 – ₹5,00,000
  • Tarun: Loans ₹5,00,001 – ₹10,00,000

Collateral-free loans for micro/small entrepreneurs, women, and self-employed individuals.

  • Eligibility: Indian citizens with viable business plans
  • Documents: Aadhaar, PAN, business plan, income proof

🏭 PMEGP – Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme

A flagship scheme to generate employment through establishment of micro-enterprises in rural and urban areas.

  • Up to ₹25L for manufacturing, ₹10L for services
  • Subsidy up to 35% based on location and category
  • Eligibility: 18+ years, minimum 8th standard pass
  • Documents: Aadhaar, PAN, educational certificate, project report

🧍‍♀️ Stand-Up India Scheme

Encourages women and SC/ST entrepreneurs by providing bank loans between ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore for setting up enterprises.

  • For manufacturing, service, or trading sector
  • At least 51% stake to be held by eligible applicant
  • Eligibility: SC/ST or Women entrepreneur aged 18+
  • Documents: Aadhaar, PAN, caste certificate (if applicable), project plan

🛡️ Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS)

Collateral-free loans to MSMEs, business enterprises, and individual borrowers for COVID-19 related distress relief.

  • Loan up to 20% of existing credit
  • No collateral, 100% government guarantee
  • Eligibility: Existing borrowers with good repayment history
  • Documents: Aadhaar, PAN, loan sanction letter, business proof
